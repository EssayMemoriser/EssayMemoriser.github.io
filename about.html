Purpose

A clean, distraction-free tool to learn essays efficiently using chunking, cloze prompts, recall, and periodic cumulative recalls.

Features

  • One-sentence training with optional cloze.
  • Cumulative recall after N sentences.
  • Smart checking (order-flexible LCS matching) with side-by-side diff.
  • Mobile-friendly controls and theme options.
  • Library for saving/loading essays locally.

Credits & License

Built as a lightweight, client-only web app. You control your data (stored locally in your browser). Feel free to adapt to your needs.
