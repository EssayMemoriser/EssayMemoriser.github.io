How it works

Stages

  • Read – Read the current sentence (or paragraph).
  • Cloze – Optional masked prompt to test recognition.
  • Recall – Type from memory and click Check.
  • Cumulative – After N sentences, recall everything so far.

Controls

  • Prev/Next – Navigate stages and chunks.
  • Show/Hide – Peek during Recall stages.
  • Copy chunk – Copy current chunk to clipboard.
  • 🔊 Read – Text-to-speech of the current prompt.
  • ⛶ Fullscreen – Focus mode.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Cmd/Ctrl + Enter – Check
  • ← / → – Prev / Next

Tips for memorisation

  • Chunk wisely – Smaller chunks help accuracy. Increase later.
  • Use Cloze early – Recognition helps prime recall.
  • Repeat cumulative – The more cumulative recalls, the deeper it sticks.
  • Read aloud – Use TTS or your own voice to encode through speech.

Troubleshooting

  • No styles? – Hard refresh (Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+R). Ensure you opened Engshit/index.html.
  • TTS not working? – Your browser must support speechSynthesis.
  • Lost essay? – Essays and settings auto-save locally. Use Export to back up.
